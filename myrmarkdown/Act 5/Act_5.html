2. Starting from the previous plot, adjust the BMI axis to show only labels for 20, 30, 40 and the weight axis to show breaks for 60 to 100 in steps of 5, adding the units (kilograms) to the axis label. Look at the help page for “scale_x_continuous” and “scale_y_continuous”

ggplot(patients,aes(x = BMI, y = Weight))+
  geom_point()+
  scale_x_continuous(
    limits = c(20,40),
    breaks = c(20,30,40))+
  scale_y_continuous(
    limits =  c(60,100),
    breaks = seq(60,100,by = 5),
